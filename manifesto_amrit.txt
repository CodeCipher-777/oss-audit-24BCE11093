
Open Source Manifesto — Amrit Raj
Date: 26 March 2026
-------------------------------

Every day, I rely on python3 — a tool built freely by people who believed
that knowledge should be shared, not locked away. To me, freedom means choice.
That is the spirit of open source: the choice to use, study, modify and share.
If I could build anything and share it with the world, I would build a free education app —
and I would release it openly, so others could learn from it, improve it,
and pass it forward. Open source is not just a license. It is a promise.

— Amrit Raj, Open Source Software Course, 26 March 2026
